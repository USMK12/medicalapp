1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data entered by authorized medical professionals (doctors) for the purpose of calculating patient prognosis scores. This information may include patient health data and other related information necessary for accurate prognosis calculations.

2. Purpose of Data Collection and Usage

This app was developed for educational and research purposes, particularly for use in doctor theses and academic studies. Data entered into the app will be used exclusively to assist doctors in calculating prognosis scores for patients. No data will be shared or disclosed to external parties under any circumstances.

3. Roles and Responsibilities

- **Admin**: The admin has access to manage doctor accounts within the app but does not access patient data. Admin functions are limited to administrative and security management.
- **Doctor**: Authorized doctors are responsible for adding and managing patient data in a secure environment and for calculating prognosis scores.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to ensure all data within the app remains private and confidential. Access to data is restricted to authorized users (admins and doctors), and all data is stored securely. We take precautions to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure and follow industry standards to protect patient information.

5. Data Sharing

No data collected within this app will be shared with any third parties. Patient information is stored securely and will only be used for the intended purpose of prognosis calculation. This ensures that patient privacy is maintained at all times.

6. User Rights

Patients and doctors have the right to request access to, update, or delete any data they have entered. If you need to exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be made available on this page, and significant updates will be communicated to users directly.
